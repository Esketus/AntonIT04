    • Fabio Wibmer joins the Canyon family!

    Next to the Sender and the Torque we equipped the Austrian prodigy with a new trials bike, exclusively designed for him. “I am very happy to have found a new partner in Canyon, which supplies me not only with an enduro and a downhill bike, but also with a trials bike, and the quality of the Canyon bikes is self-explanatory,” adds the young bike all-rounder.

    At only 24 years of age, his big lines and his amazing tricks, have already made Fabio Wibmer a big name in the bike scene due to his fearless appearance. This past year alone, he stopped the bike world in their tracks with two breathtaking video projects: “Wibmer’s Law” and the third installment of the “Urban Freeride Lives” series pushed the visits of his YouTube-channel to incredible heights.

    “I am extremely pleased to welcome Fabio to Canyon”, says Roman Arnold, founder and CEO of Canyon Bicycles. “He is an extraordinary character, with a calm demeanor that belies an incredible work ethic coupled with maximum fun on the bike. It is exactly this spirit that engages such a huge audience, that includes me!”

    Fabio will not only be riding Canyon bikes, his word and opinion will carry weight within our R&D department and also be of importance for future projects: “I am looking forward to our close cooperation in the development of new products such as a trial bike designed especially for Fabio, but also to developing the bikes throughout our Gravity world,” continues Roman.

    We are excited to welcome Fabio to the Canyon family!
